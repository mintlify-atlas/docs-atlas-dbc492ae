This guide will walk you through installing ThystTV and watching your first Twitch stream with all the enhanced features.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- An Android device running Android 6.0 or higher
- A Twitch account (optional, but recommended for following channels)
- An internet connection

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download ThystTV">
    Download the latest APK from the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/tzii/ThystTV/releases/latest):
    
    1. Tap the `ThystTV-{version}.apk` file to download
    2. Wait for the download to complete
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Install the app">
    1. Open the downloaded APK file
    2. If prompted, enable "Install unknown apps" for your browser
    3. Tap **Install**
    4. Tap **Open** when installation completes
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  See the [Installation guide](/installation) for detailed instructions and troubleshooting.
</Tip>

## First-time setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch ThystTV">
    Open ThystTV from your app drawer. You'll see the home screen with featured streams and top categories.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Log in to Twitch (optional)">
    To access your followed channels and enable chat features:
    
    1. Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner
    2. Tap **Sign in with Twitch**
    3. Log in with your Twitch credentials
    4. Authorize ThystTV to access your account
    
    <Note>
      You can browse and watch streams without logging in, but you won't be able to send chat messages or see your followed channels.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse streams">
    Explore available content:
    
    - **Home tab**: Browse featured streams and top categories
    - **Search**: Tap the search icon to find specific channels or games
    - **Following tab**: See your followed channels (requires login)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Watch your first stream

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a stream">
    Tap any live stream thumbnail to open the stream player. The stream will start playing automatically.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Try gesture controls">
    Control your viewing experience with gestures:
    
    **Volume control:**
    - Swipe up/down on the **right side** of the screen to increase/decrease volume
    
    **Brightness control:**
    - Swipe up/down on the **left side** of the screen to adjust brightness
    
    **For VODs (video on demand):**
    - Swipe left/right on the **top half** to seek backward/forward
    - Swipe left/right on the **bottom half** to adjust playback speed
    
    <Tip>
      You'll see a visual slider overlay while adjusting. Gestures are disabled when player controls are visible to prevent accidental inputs.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enable floating chat">
    Watch in full-screen while keeping up with chat:
    
    1. **Double-tap** anywhere on the screen to cycle chat modes:
       - Overlay mode (chat floats on top)
       - Side-by-side mode (split screen)
       - Hidden (no chat visible)
    
    2. In overlay mode:
       - **Drag** the chat window to reposition it
       - **Pinch** to resize the chat window
       - Tap the opacity icon to adjust transparency
       - Tap the contrast icon for high contrast mode
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check your watch statistics">
    ThystTV automatically tracks your viewing time:
    
    1. Tap the **Stats** tab in the bottom navigation
    2. View your daily viewing time
    3. See your top watched channels
    4. Explore category breakdowns with charts
    
    <Note>
      All statistics are stored locally on your device and never shared or uploaded.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customize your experience

### Gesture settings

Adjust gesture behavior to your preference:

1. Tap the menu icon (☰) → **Settings**
2. Navigate to **Player** → **Gesture Controls**
3. Customize:
   - **Gesture sensitivity**: Adjust how much movement triggers actions
   - **Zone boundaries**: Configure top/bottom and left/right split points
   - **Haptic feedback**: Enable vibration feedback (disabled by default)

### Chat appearance

Customize the chat overlay:

1. Open any stream with chat
2. In overlay mode, use the chat controls:
   - **Opacity slider**: Adjust chat transparency
   - **High contrast toggle**: Improve readability over bright content
   - **Font size**: Change in Settings → **Chat**

### Player settings

Optimize video playback:

1. Go to Settings → **Player**
2. Configure:
   - **Default quality**: Auto, Source, 1080p60, 720p60, etc.
   - **Low latency mode**: Reduce stream delay
   - **Picture-in-picture**: Enable background playback

## Common tasks

### Follow a channel

1. Open any stream or channel page
2. Tap the heart icon to follow
3. Access followed channels in the **Following** tab

<Warning>
  You must be logged in to follow channels.
</Warning>

### Watch a VOD

1. Go to a channel page
2. Tap the **Videos** tab
3. Select a past broadcast or highlight
4. Use seek gestures (swipe left/right on top half)
5. Adjust playback speed (swipe left/right on bottom half)

### Enable picture-in-picture

1. Start playing a stream
2. Tap the home button or switch apps
3. The stream continues in a floating window
4. Tap the window to return to full screen

### Clear watch history

1. Go to Settings → **Privacy**
2. Tap **Clear watch statistics**
3. Confirm the action

<Warning>
  This permanently deletes all locally stored viewing data. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Stream won't load

- Check your internet connection
- Try lowering the quality in Settings → Player
- Restart the app

### Gestures not working

- Ensure player controls are hidden (tap screen once to hide)
- Check that gestures are enabled in Settings → Player
- Adjust sensitivity settings if gestures are too sensitive or not sensitive enough

### Chat overlay disappears

- Double-tap to cycle chat modes back to overlay
- Check that you're logged in for full chat functionality

### Statistics not showing

- Watch at least one stream for a few minutes to generate data
- Stats update in real-time while watching
