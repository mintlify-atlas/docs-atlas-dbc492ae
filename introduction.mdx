## Welcome to ThystTV

ThystTV is a feature-rich third-party Twitch client for Android that enhances your viewing experience with floating chat, comprehensive watch statistics, and intuitive gesture controls. Built as a fork of [Xtra](https://github.com/crackededed/Xtra), ThystTV adds powerful features focused on viewer experience, data management, and accessibility.

<Note>
  ThystTV is experimental and features are tested manually. While the app is fully functional, some features may have rough edges. Contributions and bug reports are welcome!
</Note>

### Floating chat overlay

Watch streams in full-screen while keeping up with chat using the floating overlay feature:

- **Overlay mode**: Chat appears as a resizable window on top of the stream
- **Customizable positioning**: Move the chat window anywhere on screen
- **Opacity control**: Adjust transparency to balance visibility between stream and chat
- **High contrast mode**: Toggle for better readability over bright video content
- **Double-tap switching**: Cycle through overlay, side-by-side, and hidden chat modes

### Watch statistics

Monitor your viewing habits with comprehensive statistics:

- **Daily tracking**: View your daily viewing time directly in the app
- **Top channels**: See a leaderboard of your most-watched channels and streamers
- **Category breakdown**: Charts and legends showing what you watch most
- **Data visualization**: Bar charts, pie charts, and heatmaps for insights
- **Stats tab**: Easy access via the bottom navigation bar

### Gesture controls

Control your viewing experience without blocking the screen:

- **Volume**: Slide up/down on the right half of the screen
- **Brightness**: Slide up/down on the left half of the screen
- **Seek** (VoD only): Horizontal swipe on the top half of the screen
- **Playback speed** (VoD only): Horizontal swipe on the bottom half of the screen
- **Visual feedback**: Real-time slider overlay during adjustments
- **Edge protection**: Gestures disabled in system gesture zones to prevent conflicts
- **Customizable**: Adjust sensitivity, zones, and haptic feedback in Settings

<Tip>
  Gestures are automatically disabled when player controls are visible to prevent accidental inputs.
</Tip>

### Privacy first

Your data belongs to you:

- All watch statistics are stored locally on your device
- No analytics or tracking services
- No cloud synchronization or data uploads
- No third-party data sharing
