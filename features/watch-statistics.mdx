Watch Statistics provides comprehensive insights into your ThystTV viewing habits, including daily screen time, watch streaks, category breakdowns, and streamer loyalty metrics.

## Accessing Statistics

View your watch statistics from the main menu:

1. Tap the menu icon (≡) in the top-left corner
2. Select **Statistics** from the navigation menu
3. Your stats dashboard will load with all available data

## Screen Time Overview

### Daily Bar Chart

The daily bar chart shows your watch time for the past 7 days:

- **Today**: Highlighted with a "Today" label
- **Previous days**: Labeled with day abbreviations (Mon, Tue, etc.)
- **Bar height**: Represents total minutes watched that day
- **Interactive**: Tap any bar to see exact watch time

### Key Metrics

<Accordion title="Daily Average">
  Your average watch time per day over the past week, calculated from the last 7 days of data. Displayed in hours and minutes format (e.g., "2 hr 45 min").
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Today's Time">
  Total time spent watching streams and videos today. Updates in real-time as you watch content.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Week Total">
  Combined watch time across all 7 days shown in the bar chart. Helps you understand your weekly viewing patterns.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Week Change">
  Comparison with the previous week, showing whether your watch time increased or decreased (e.g., "↑ from last week").
</Accordion>

## Watch Streaks

Track your consistency with daily watch streaks:

### Current Streak

The number of consecutive days you've watched content on ThystTV. A day counts toward your streak if you watch for any amount of time.

### Longest Streak

Your all-time record for consecutive days of watching. This milestone is saved permanently.

<Tip>
Watch at least a few minutes every day to maintain your streak! Even brief viewing sessions count.
</Tip>

<Note>
Streaks reset at midnight in your local timezone. Make sure to open a stream before the day ends to keep your streak alive.
</Note>

## Category Breakdown

### Pie Chart Visualization

The category pie chart displays your watch time distribution across Twitch categories (games) for the last 30 days:

- **Color-coded slices**: Each category has a unique color
- **Size indicates time**: Larger slices = more time spent watching that category
- **Percentage labels**: Shows what portion of your total watch time went to each category

### Category Legend

Below the pie chart, a scrollable legend lists all categories with:

- **Category name**: The game or category title
- **Total time**: Hours and minutes spent watching
- **Percentage**: Share of your total watch time
- **Color indicator**: Matches the pie chart slice

<Accordion title="Why are some categories showing as 'Unknown'?">
  If a stream didn't have a category set or the category data wasn't available, it appears as "Unknown" in the breakdown. This typically happens with older VODs or streams that changed categories mid-broadcast.
</Accordion>

## Hourly Breakdown

The heatmap visualization shows which hours of the day you typically watch content:

### Reading the Heatmap

- **Horizontal axis**: Hours of the day (0-23, using 24-hour format)
- **Color intensity**: Darker colors indicate more watch time during that hour
- **Data range**: Based on the last 30 days

### Use Cases

- **Identify peak hours**: See when you watch the most
- **Plan your schedule**: Understand your viewing patterns
- **Track changes**: Notice shifts in your daily routine

<Tip>
The heatmap uses your local timezone, so "12" represents noon in your area.
</Tip>

## Top Streams

A ranked list of the streamers you've watched the most:

### Metrics Shown

- **Rank**: Position in your top streams list (1-10)
- **Streamer name**: Channel name or display name
- **Total watch time**: Cumulative time spent watching this streamer
- **Stream count**: Number of different streams/sessions watched

### Sorting

Streams are sorted by total watch time in descending order, with your most-watched streamer at the top.

## Streamer Loyalty

Identifies streamers you consistently watch:

### Loyalty Metrics

- **Consistency**: How regularly you watch this streamer
- **Recent activity**: Whether you've watched them in the past week
- **Total sessions**: Number of times you've tuned in

<Note>
Streamer loyalty is calculated based on both total watch time and viewing frequency. Watching a streamer regularly, even for shorter periods, can rank higher than occasional marathon sessions.
</Note>

## Data Privacy

All watch statistics are stored **locally on your device**:

- No data is sent to external servers
- Statistics are private and never shared
- You can clear your history anytime from Settings
- Data persists across app updates

## Clearing Statistics

To reset your watch statistics:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Data & Privacy**
2. Tap **Clear Watch History**
3. Confirm the action

<Warning>
Clearing watch statistics is permanent and cannot be undone. All metrics, including streaks and category data, will be reset to zero.
</Warning>

## Technical Details

### What Counts as Watch Time

- Only active playback counts toward statistics
- Paused or buffering time is excluded
- Background playback (audio-only) counts as watch time
- Minimum increment: 1 second

### Data Collection

ThystTV tracks:

- Start and end time of viewing sessions
- Channel/streamer information
- Category/game being streamed
- Video type (live stream, VOD, clip)

### Storage Limits

Statistics are stored indefinitely unless manually cleared. The database uses minimal storage (typically under 1 MB even with months of data).

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Statistics aren't updating">
  Try these steps:
  1. Make sure you're watching content for at least a few seconds
  2. Close and reopen the Statistics page to refresh
  3. Check that you're running the latest version of ThystTV
  4. If issues persist, try clearing app cache (Settings > Storage)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Missing historical data">
  Watch statistics began tracking after you first installed ThystTV. Data before installation or before enabling the feature is not available.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Inaccurate watch time">
  Watch time is calculated from playback telemetry. If you notice discrepancies:
  - Ensure your device clock is set correctly
  - Check that the app isn't being force-stopped by battery optimization
  - Very brief viewing sessions (under 5 seconds) may not be recorded
</Accordion>
