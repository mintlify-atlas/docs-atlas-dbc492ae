---
title: Floating Chat
description: Overlay chat on top of the video stream for an immersive viewing experience
---

Floating Chat allows you to position and resize a chat overlay anywhere on the video player while watching streams in landscape mode. This feature keeps you connected with the community without sacrificing screen space.

## Enabling Floating Chat

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the player in landscape mode">
    Start watching a stream and rotate your device to landscape orientation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle chat mode">
    Tap the chat toggle button in the player controls to cycle through chat modes:
    - **Sidebar**: Chat appears in a sidebar (default)
    - **Floating**: Chat appears as a draggable overlay
    - **Hidden**: Chat is completely hidden
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Position the floating chat">
    Once in floating mode, drag the chat overlay anywhere on the screen by touching and moving it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Floating chat is only available in landscape mode. When you rotate to portrait, chat automatically returns to its standard position below the video.
</Note>

## Customizing Floating Chat

### Positioning

The floating chat overlay can be freely positioned anywhere on the screen:

1. Touch and hold the chat overlay
2. Drag it to your preferred position
3. Release to drop it in place

Your position is automatically saved per channel, so each stream remembers where you placed the chat.

### Resizing

Adjust the chat size to fit your viewing preference:

1. Touch and hold the resize handle in the bottom-right corner of the chat overlay
2. Drag to increase or decrease the chat size
3. Release when you've reached the desired size

The chat dimensions are also saved per channel.

<Tip>
For streams with fast-moving chat, a larger chat window helps you keep up with the conversation.
</Tip>

### Transparency Settings

Control the chat overlay's transparency:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Player** > **Floating Chat**
2. Adjust the **Transparency** slider
3. Higher transparency lets you see more of the video through the chat

### High Visibility Mode

When enabled, High Visibility mode makes chat text easier to read over video content:

- **Bold text**: All messages appear in bold
- **Text shadows**: Adds subtle shadows to improve contrast
- **Enhanced readability**: Ideal for bright or busy video backgrounds

To enable:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Player** > **Floating Chat**
2. Toggle **High Visibility** on

<Accordion title="Why isn't floating chat appearing?">
  Floating chat requires:
  - Landscape orientation
  - Maximized player (not minimized/picture-in-picture)
  - Floating chat enabled in settings
  
  Check **Settings** > **Player** > **Floating Chat** to ensure it's enabled.
</Accordion>

## Persistence

Floating chat remembers your preferences:

- **Position**: Saved per channel
- **Size**: Saved per channel  
- **Transparency**: Global setting
- **High visibility**: Global setting
- **Chat mode**: Remembered across app sessions

When you return to a channel, the floating chat will appear exactly where you left it.

## Interactions

### Reading Chat

- **Auto-scroll**: Chat automatically scrolls to show new messages
- **Manual scroll**: Scroll up to read message history
- **Scroll to bottom**: Tap the down arrow button to jump to the latest messages

### Sending Messages

Floating chat is read-only. To send messages:

1. Toggle back to sidebar mode, or
2. Minimize the player to access the full chat interface

<Note>
Floating chat is optimized for viewing. For full chat interaction including emotes, replies, and moderation actions, use sidebar mode.
</Note>

## Performance Tips

- Smaller chat overlays use less system resources
- High visibility mode may slightly impact performance on older devices
- Adjust transparency based on your video content—darker videos work better with higher transparency

## Disabling Floating Chat

To disable floating chat entirely:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Player** > **Floating Chat**
2. Toggle **Enable Floating Chat** off

When disabled, the chat toggle button will only cycle between sidebar and hidden modes.
